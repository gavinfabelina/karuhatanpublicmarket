ABOUT US

Welcome to Karuhatan's very own market!

Experience your Karuhatan Public Market

Since its establishment, the Karuhatan Public Market have served as the central hub of community activity and commerce. It provides access to fresh produce, household goods, staples, and other items. Over the years, the market has evolved into a bustling marketplace, where a variety of vendors has been catered to come together to showcase their products.

At Karuhatan Public Market, our mission is simple yet profound: to serve as a catalyst for economic growth, cultural exchange and community development. As we continue to grow and serve the needs of the community, we remain committed to preserve a welcoming environment for all. Join us at the Karuhatan Public Market, where every stall brims with quality goods and every visit is an opportunity to discover something new.
